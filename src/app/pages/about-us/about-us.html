Our Story

A reliable supplier of all kind of metals

Umiya Steel and Impex began over a decade ago with a vision to provide high-quality metal products to industries across the globe. Founded by Rohit Mehta, a passionate industry expert, our journey started with a commitment to excellence, integrity, and customer-centricity. Over the years, we have grown from a small local supplier to a renowned name in the metals industry, known for our comprehensive range of products and exceptional service.

From the outset, we recognized the importance of building strong relationships with our clients. Our dedication to understanding their unique needs and delivering tailored solutions has been the cornerstone of our success. As we expanded our product line to include aluminum, stainless steel, copper, brass, galvanized, and mild steel, we ensured that quality and reliability remained at the forefront of everything we do.
